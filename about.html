About Ben Hawes

Ben Hawes is a speaker and consultant specializing in employee activation — helping organizations transform passive participation into energized ownership.

His career spans high-growth startups, leadership development environments, live event production, and stage performance. Across each chapter, one insight became undeniable:

Strategy sets direction. Activation determines execution.

Inside companies like ClassPass and Rocket Money, Ben worked alongside managers navigating scale, pressure, and performance expectations. He saw how quickly morale shifts affect output — and how rarely teams have language to diagnose disengagement before it spreads.

As a training leader, he helped managers strengthen accountability and communication. As the founder of Rent My Booth, he built a corporate events company focused on increasing engagement at high-impact moments.

As a performer and host, he learned to read rooms instantly — adjusting tone, pacing, and delivery in real time. That instinct now informs his consulting and keynote work.

Ben does not advise on company strategy. He transforms how people engage with it.

Outside of his activation work, Ben hosts the Life in Bold podcast and performs original comedy music.
