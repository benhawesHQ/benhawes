Strategy is clear. Momentum is harder.

Inside growing organizations, the strategy is usually thoughtful and well-defined. Leadership teams spend months shaping the roadmap, aligning priorities, and setting ambitious goals for the next quarter or year.

But translating that strategy into daily energy across the team is a different challenge.

People get busy. Communication gets fragmented. Priorities compete with each other. Slowly, the energy behind the strategy fades.

Ben’s keynote creates a moment where teams reconnect to the work directly in front of them and move forward with renewed momentum.

The Activation Model

A framework for turning strategy into momentum.

Leaders invest enormous energy into building thoughtful strategy. Clear goals, roadmaps, and priorities shape the direction of the organization.

But strategy only succeeds when teams feel clear, connected, and energized enough to execute it.

In this keynote, Ben introduces a simple model teams can use to evaluate and strengthen their engagement with the work ahead.

Clarity

Do people understand the priorities, goals, and strategy guiding the work ahead?

Communication

Are teams having open conversations about priorities, collaboration, and expectations?

Commitment

Do people feel energized enough to move the work forward?
