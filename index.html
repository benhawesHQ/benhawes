Activate Your Team

The problem isn’t strategy.

Leaders design strategy all the time. Vision decks are built. Roadmaps are defined. OKRs are set.

But unless your people understand how their day-to-day work connects to that vision, the strategy will not go the distance.

Engagement is not happiness. It is not satisfaction. Engagement is commitment. Effort. Ownership. It is the willingness to try harder because the work feels meaningful.

And most companies do not know how to measure it, communicate about it, or activate it.

Activation is the bridge between vision and results.

For over fifteen years, Ben has helped growing companies translate big ideas into lived experience.

At ClassPass, he led learning and development as the company scaled from 200 to 700 employees. At LifeLabs Learning, he partnered with HR leaders and executives to design measurable training programs. At Rocket Money, he shapes the day-to-day experience of a fast-moving tech office.

He has worked across customer service, marketing, engineering, sales, operations, and leadership teams.

His reputation inside organizations has been consistent: the person who can step into the room, clarify the message, elevate the energy, and help people commit to the mission.
