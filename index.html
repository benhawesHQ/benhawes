About Ben

Ben Hawes is a team activation speaker who believes the most powerful driver of execution is emotional connection to the mission. His work focuses on helping organizations translate strategy into energy across teams.

After leading learning and development initiatives at ClassPass and supporting culture and engagement programs inside scaling organizations, Ben developed frameworks that help teams reconnect to purpose, clarity, and commitment.

Today he works with companies to activate teams around strategy through storytelling, shared experiences, and practical frameworks leaders can immediately apply.
