Inside most organizations, strategy is not the problem. Leaders spend months building roadmaps, priorities, and goals. But somewhere between the leadership team and the day-to-day work of employees, the energy behind those ideas gets lost.

Ben Hawes delivers keynotes designed to change that moment. His talks help teams reconnect to the mission, understand the bigger picture, and rediscover the energy that turns strategy into movement.

Bring Ben to Your Team

The challenge inside modern organizations

Most employees want to care deeply about their work. They want to feel connected to the mission of the company and understand how their effort contributes to something meaningful.

But as companies grow, priorities multiply. Teams become specialized. Communication becomes fragmented. And slowly the connection between the strategy at the top and the work happening across the organization begins to fade.

Not because people don’t care. But because the link between their work and the bigger mission becomes harder to see.

That’s where activation matters.

The moment when everything clicks

During Ben’s keynote, something powerful happens in the room. Employees begin to see the larger story of the company again. Leaders hear the language their teams use to talk about motivation and alignment. And suddenly the room shifts.

There is more laughter. More honesty. More energy.

People begin to realize they are not just completing tasks. They are building something meaningful together.

The Activation Keynote

The keynote introduces a simple framework that helps teams reconnect their daily work to the mission of the organization.

Clarity

Participants step back and see the broader strategy of the company. They understand how their individual work contributes to the success of the organization.

Communication

Teams learn a shared language for discussing engagement, alignment, and priorities. This creates space for more honest and productive conversations between employees and leaders.

Commitment

Participants reflect on how they personally show up inside the company and leave with renewed ownership of the mission ahead.

Meet Ben Hawes

Ben Hawes has spent more than a decade helping organizations activate their people.

His career spans leadership and engagement roles at companies like ClassPass, LifeLabs Learning, and Rocket Money where he designed learning programs, built leadership development systems, and helped teams reconnect to the mission of the organizations they were building.

Alongside his work in learning and development, Ben runs a corporate events company focused on team engagement experiences. Through that work he has partnered with organizations including Uber, Meta, Framebridge, Macy’s, Brooklyn Museum, HelloFresh, WeWork, and Faire.

Today he brings those experiences into keynote talks designed to help teams reconnect to strategy, communicate openly about engagement, and build momentum around the work that matters most.
