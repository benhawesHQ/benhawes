The Engagement Activation Model

Clarity + Communication = Commitment

Clarity

Employees clearly understand how their day-to-day work connects to the company mission and goals.

Communication

Teams feel comfortable discussing motivation, challenges, and alignment with leadership.

Commitment

When clarity and communication exist, teams commit to delivering the strategy.
