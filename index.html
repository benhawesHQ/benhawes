The Activation Model

Clarity + Communication = Commitment

Clarity

Employees understand how their day-to-day work connects to the larger company mission and objectives.

Communication

Teams are able to openly discuss motivation, challenges, and alignment with leadership.

Commitment

When clarity and communication exist, employees commit to delivering results.

Meet Ben Hawes

Ben Hawes is a leadership and engagement speaker who specializes in activating the motivation of teams large and small.

He spent five years at ClassPass building learning and development programs, followed by three years at LifeLabs Learning specializing in leadership and engagement training.

He now leads employee experience and workplace engagement initiatives in New York at Rocket Money.

Outside the corporate world, Ben is a performer, musician, and comedian. That experience brings humor and energy to his speaking sessions, creating highly interactive talks that leave audiences feeling lighter, motivated, and ready to act.
