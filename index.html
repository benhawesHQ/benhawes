The strategy is clear.
The challenge is execution.

Leadership teams spend enormous effort defining strategy and aligning priorities. But turning that strategy into daily momentum across a team is harder. People get busy. Communication fragments. Priorities compete. Slowly the connection between strategy and everyday work fades. Not because people don’t care. Because the link between the mission and the work directly in front of them becomes harder to see.
