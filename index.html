The Activation Framework

Most organizations have strategy, goals, and a clear direction.

But strategy alone doesn't move work forward. Activated people do.

This keynote helps teams reconnect their daily work to the mission of the organization and gives them a structure for honest conversations about commitment, ownership, and progress.

Clarity

Employees understand how their individual work connects to the organization's mission and strategic direction.

Communication

Teams learn how to talk openly with managers about priorities, goals, and engagement with the work ahead.

Activation

Employees reflect on how they show up at work and leave with a renewed sense of ownership and commitment.

What's Your Activation Score?

At the center of Ben's keynote is a simple reflection question:

How activated are you in your work right now?

Participants rate themselves on a scale from 1 to 10 and reflect on a simple question:

What would move you one point higher?

That reflection becomes the starting point for more honest conversations between employees and managers about goals, motivation, and ownership.
