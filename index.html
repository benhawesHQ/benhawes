About Ben

Ben Hawes has spent more than a decade working inside organizations focused on engagement, training, and team culture. His career spans roles in customer experience, leadership development, and employee training at companies like ClassPass, LifeLabs Learning, and Rocket Money. Along the way he became fascinated with a simple question: what actually makes teams feel connected to their work? Today his keynote brings together those experiences with storytelling and humor to create a moment where teams pause, reconnect to their mission, and feel excited about the work ahead.
