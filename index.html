About Ben

Ben Hawes is a team activation and engagement speaker who believes the most powerful driver of execution is emotional connection to the mission.

His work focuses on aligning strategy with energy across teams through clarity, communication, and commitment.

After leading learning and development initiatives at ClassPass and supporting global culture and engagement programs at LifeLabs Learning, Ben developed frameworks that help teams align around purpose and execute together.

Today he works with organizations to activate teams around strategy through storytelling, shared experiences, and practical frameworks that leaders and team members can apply immediately.
