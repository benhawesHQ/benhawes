About Ben

Ben Hawes is a team activation and engagement speaker who believes the most powerful driver of execution is emotional connection to the mission.

After leading learning and development initiatives at ClassPass and supporting global culture programs at LifeLabs Learning, Ben developed frameworks that help teams align around clarity, communication, and commitment.

Today he works with organizations to activate teams around strategy through storytelling, shared experiences, and practical frameworks leaders and team members can apply immediately.
