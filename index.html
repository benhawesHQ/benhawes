Engaged is one thing. Activated goes further.

Most companies spend enormous time developing strategy, aligning goals, and planning for the future. But strategy alone doesn't move work forward. People do. Ben Hawes helps organizations activate the people responsible for bringing strategy to life.

The Activation Framework

Clarity

People understand the direction of the organization and how their work connects to it.

Communication

Employees and managers have honest conversations about expectations, priorities, and goals.

Activation

People choose to step forward, take ownership, and commit to the work ahead.

What's Your Activation Score?

Participants leave Ben's keynote with a simple reflection: How activated are you in your work right now?
