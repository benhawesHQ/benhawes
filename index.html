About Ben

Ben Hawes is a team activation keynote speaker who helps organizations reconnect strategy with the human energy required to execute it.

After leading learning initiatives at ClassPass and supporting global culture and engagement programs at LifeLabs Learning, Ben developed frameworks that help teams align around clarity, communication, and commitment.

Today he works with organizations to activate teams around strategy through storytelling, shared experiences, and practical frameworks that leaders and employees can apply immediately.
