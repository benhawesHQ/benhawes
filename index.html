What's Your Activation Score?

At the center of Ben’s keynote is one simple question.

How activated are you in your work right now?

Participants rate themselves on a scale from 1 to 10 and reflect on a simple question:

What would move you one point higher?

10

Fully committed, energized, and taking ownership.

7–9

Engaged and contributing, but holding something back.

4–6

Doing the work, but not fully invested in the direction.

1–3

Disconnected from the mission or unclear about the path forward.
